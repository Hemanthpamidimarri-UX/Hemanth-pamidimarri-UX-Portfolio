My story

Spatial thinker turned product designer

After years of designing physical spaces — understanding how people move through rooms, what frustrates them, how they find what they need — I realized these were exactly the questions product design asks, just on a screen instead of a floor plan.

The transition wasn't a pivot away from what I loved. It was a zoom-in. Every wireframe I draw is a floor plan. Every user flow is a spatial journey. My interior design background gives me an instinct for hierarchy, flow, and the emotional weight of empty space that most UX designers develop only over years.

I'm based in Kondapalli, Andhra Pradesh — open to remote roles and Hyderabad-based opportunities.
